Gray Wolf

The gray wolf is one of most acknowledged species of wolves in the entire world!

  • Scientific Name : Canis lupus.
  • Vertebrate class : mammals.
  • Diet : Carnivores.
  • Breeding Time : Between January and March.

The color of their coat is usually a mixture of brown and gray. Their average body size is three to five feet long. Males usually weigh from 70 to 145 pounds, while females weigh from 60 to 100 pounds.
